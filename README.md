# Ball Brawlers 🟢⚔️

A fast, physics-y **2D ball-brawler roguelite** — pick a ball, bounce around the
arena, grab weapons, unleash your ability, and battle through a campaign of
deadlier and deadlier opponents. **Free to play.**

## ⬇️ Download & Play
- 🪟 **Windows:** grab the latest **[Release](../../releases/latest)**, download
  `ball-brawlers-windows.zip`, unzip, and run `BallGame.exe`.
- 🌐 **Browser / phone:** playable on **itch.io** — _link coming soon_.
- 🤖 **Android:** `.apk` coming soon.

## 🎮 How to play
- Your ball moves and bounces on its own — **positioning and timing** win fights.
- Press **Q** (or tap the ability button) to use your ball's special move.
- Grab **weapons** that drop into the arena — guns, melee, harpoon, shield.
- Win levels to advance the campaign; beat enemies to unlock them.
- If you lose, the run restarts — but your **ball levels, coins, gems, and
  unlocked balls are kept forever.** Updates never wipe your progress.

## ✨ What's inside
- **8+ balls**, each with its own ability — Knight, Queen, Assassin, Ninja,
  Shark, Viking, King, and the splitting Slime boss.
- **Passives & unlock quests** — the King enrages at low HP; the Viking goes
  berserk; hunt a Viking *as the Shark* to unlock him.
- **4 stages** with mini-boss ambushes, hazards, and an apex boss.
- Roguelite progression: permanent levels, rarity cards, luck, a shop, skins,
  and weapon upgrades.
- **Hard but fair** — no hit can take more than 35% of your health, ever.
